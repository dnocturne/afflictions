# Affliction Types

Categories of afflictions in the plugin.

---

## Categories

### Physical
Affects the player's body and physical abilities.
- Movement speed
- Health/damage
- Hunger/saturation

### Mental
Affects perception and behavior.
- Vision effects
- Sound distortion
- Control interference

### Supernatural
Magical or otherworldly afflictions.
- Curses
- Magical diseases
- Entity interactions

### Environmental
Caused by world interactions.
- Biome-specific
- Block exposure
- Weather-related
